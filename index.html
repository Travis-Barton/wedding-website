How we met

Lindsey and Travis met sometime during their undergrad at CSU Monterey Bay, but thats not the most helpful statistic. It doesnt help you understand how they crossed that magic line that goes from aquantences to friends to soulmates. So lets start a little later at a better known event in time. The first dinner that Travis and Lindsey shared was her 18th birthday in 2013. Two of lindsey's friends needed a ride Lindsey was gracious enough to let Travis tag along.*** They met, they enjoyed each others company, and they parted ways.
The timing wasn't right yet.


In 2014, Travis got the chance to go surfing with Lindsey, He jumped on it! And at the end of the night, he worked up the courage to ask for her number, and she said yes!*

They continued to grow closer. Slowly dancing towards that inevitable future that would be their falling-in-love, but they took the time to truely become friends first. In summer of 2016, Travis and Lindsey decided that it was time to take that jump and give the o'd dating dating thing a shot. It was instantly a hit and they were inseperable.

"You're going to love me, you know" Lindsey once told Travis inside her apartment on ____ str. and moves. Travis landed in San Jose, and Lindsey was in Santa Cruz. Travis said "maybe", but the signs where already there.** They continued to share birthdays, graduations, During covid, they graduated with their masters, and Lindsey moved to San Carlos where Travis soon followed.

Here are a few of the highlights of their adventures together: -> During an early date, Travis taught Lindsey how to longboard, something they spent a lot of time doing together around the streets of CSUMB. A less fun fact is that he forgot to tell her how to stop before sending her down a hill. The SpongeBob band aids were worth it. -> @Lindsey whatcha feeling here? :)

Then on February 10th, 2024 Travis and Lindsey went on a pici-nic at the beach. They took a basket to the coast full of fruits, meats, cheeses, and honey. They sat and ate and watched the ocean before exploring a hidden beach. While Lindsey was metal detecting, she found a mysterious box. She opened it to discover a bouquet of memories and a ring. She said yes, and now we'd like to invite you to celebrate with us.

Love, Travis and Lindsey
