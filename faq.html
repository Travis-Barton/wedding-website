Have a question?

How can I get to the venue?

You are welcome to drive yourself to Spring Ranch Mendocino for each event; parking is plentiful. A shuttle service is provided to and from the room blocks for the wedding day. Stay tuned for shuttle pick-up times!


Please note that Ubers and Lyfts are limited in the area,
we don’t recommend relying on these services.

What can I expect for the Welcome Party?

We’re excited to host a relaxed dinner of tacos, side dishes, beverages, and games to welcome you to our celebration!

Have dietary restrictions?

Don’t worry, we’re creating a menu that caters to most dietary restrictions and allergies. We will also be collecting any restrictions you have when you RSVP!

Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

Both! The ceremony, cocktail hour, and dinner will be held outside, and will move inside for dancing. Don’t worry, we will have blankets and heaters in case they’re needed for the outdoor portion.

Are kids welcome?

While we love your kids, we are making this day adult only.
Take the night off and get ready for an awesome time!

What’s the bar situation?

We’ll offer signature cocktails, mocktails, and a selection of beer and wine for your enjoyment. Water, coffee, and tea with also be available throughout the evening.

Is there an after party?

The reception ends at 10pm, but the celebration doesn’t! Join us at a local pool hall, the Welcome Inn in Fort Bragg, for a casual after-party with pizza. Shuttles will run between the after-party and hotel block, ensuring everyone gets home safely.

What is the Farewell Coffee?

We’d love for you to stop by for a casual grab-and-go brunch on your way out of town. Enjoy specialty coffee, lattes, and local pastries as we say thank you and send you off with a warm farewell!

What do I wear?

Welcome Party: Dressy Casual
Wear something you feel good in!

Wedding Day: Semi-Formal/Formal Attire
We’d love for you to wear festive, bold summer colors—go as vibrant as you’d like! Because the ceremony will be on grass, we recommend avoiding thin heels. Thank you for helping make our day extra special with your fabulous style!
Please avoid casual wear such as jeans, t-shirts or sneakers.

Farewell Coffee: Come As You Are
Wear whatever you like! We might be in pajamas ourselves!

Your presence matters most to us so don’t overthink it,
have fun and celebrate with us!
