Alternative Stays

Hotels are few in this area, but we recommend checking out one of the many local bed and breakfasts in downtown Mendocino! There are also a few glamping resorts in the area including Mendocino Grove or Heartwood Mendocino if you're feeling adventurous!

Driving Directions

Wondering what the best way to drive in is? There are a few ways to get into the area that are all scenic, though we recommend taking Highway 20 or Highway 128 as they are the most direct and least winding.

Nearest Airports

Charles M. Schulz Sonoma County Airport

Technically speaking, this is the closest airport to Mendocino. It’s located in Santa Rosa, about 100 miles and two hours away. It’s a small airport that only serves a handful of cities, including Denver, Dallas, Minneapolis, Los Angeles, Orange County, Phoenix, Portland, San Francisco, San Diego, Seattle, and Las Vegas. They might not have a lot of flight options, but if you’re coming from one of these locations, then it’s definitely the way to go!

Oakland International Airport (OAK)

Located on the East side of the San Francisco Bay, OAK is nearly 200 miles and three and a half hours away from Mendocino. This international airport has flights available from just about anywhere you may be joining us from.

San Francisco International Airport (SFO)

Located just south of San Francisco, SFO is nearly 200 miles and three and a half hours away from Mendocino. This international airport has flights available from just about anywhere you may be joining us from.

San Jose Mineta International Airport (SJC)

Located in Travis’ hometown (and many of yours), SJC is roughly 215 miles and three hours and forty-five minutes away from Mendocino. This international airport has flights available from just about anywhere you may be joining us from.
